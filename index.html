Haus Garnison...

Haus Garnison... Haus Garnison was established in 1994 and is still under the same management, this gives you the peace of mind to know that we are your home away from home. Haus Garnison is centrally located to the beach, town and other amenities.

Take a safe 5 minute’ walk into town centre and 2 minute’ walk to the beach (add a link to photo page) Haus Garnison has 10 rooms and can accommodate 18 - 21 guests. Breakfast is served daily in our dining room (add link to page of photos). You are also welcome to enjoy a coffee in our garden.

We have ample parking for tour busses and also offer garages on a 1st come 1st serve basis to our guests making use of own transport.

Haus Garnison also offers two self-catering flats for those that want to be a bit more private but still want to enjoy the services of a guest house and closeness of town and the beach (Add link to page about flats.

Self Catering beach house

Quaint beach house with ocean view, our self catering beach house gives you a bit more privacy while still remaining within walking distance of the centre of town and the beach, enjoy relaxed days in the Garden and listening to the waves while resting

Inside you'll find one bedroom with a queen size bed and en suite bathroom along with two bedrooms comprising of two single beds in each room, one bathroom. The unit consists of an entertainment area and Garden as well as a Garage for secure parking for up to two vehicles.
