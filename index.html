HAUS GARNISON is a cosy and affordable guesthouse located centrally and close to the beach in Swakopmund, Namibia

Contact us

Haus Garnison...

is very centrally situated and in about 4 minutes you can walk into the city centre and 500 meters to the beach.

Haus Garnison has 2 self-catering flats and 10 rooms and can accommodate 18 - 21 guests. All rooms are equipped with shower/bath, toilet, a mini kitchen, telephone, cooling fan & heater. Beer, wine and non alcoholic beverages can be obtained from our self-help bar facilities or served in the tranquil atmosphere of our lush garden.
